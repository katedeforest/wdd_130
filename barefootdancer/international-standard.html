International Ballroom

The International Style, which is also known as the English Style, is danced throughout the world. The dances in this more traditional style are centuries old and orginated in Europe. Although there are many variations of these dances, dance position does not fluctuate between movements. The dancers remain in closed position (the man’s left hand outstretched, the lady’s right hand placed in his hand, the man’s right hand on the lady’s left shoulder blade, and her left hand resting on his shoulder) until it is time to bow.
